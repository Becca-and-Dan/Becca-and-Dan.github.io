Both the Ceremony and Reception will be held at Woodend, Audubon Naturalist Society in Chevy Chase, Maryland

Woodend is a historic home located in Chevy Chase, Montgomery County, Maryland. This Georgian Revival house was built in 1927–1928. It is a 2 1⁄2-story house with Flemish bond brick walls and brick quoins. The house was designed by John Russell Pope, architect of the Jefferson Memorial and National Gallery of Art.

Travel Tips to Bethesda/Chevy Chase from NYC:

One of the easiest and cost effective ways to get from NYC to Bethesda is via bus. We have taken many (admittedly slightly cramped) trips on both the Vamoose and the Tripper Bus. Both leave from midtown and arrive in Bethesda (around the corner from the Hyatt) about 4.5 hours later.

Transportation to Venue:

Bus transportation will be available between the Hyatt and the wedding venue, departing the Hyatt at 5:00pm. Please check back closer in time to the wedding for more information.
